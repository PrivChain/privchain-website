Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 22, 2026

Controller and Contact

PrivChain is the data controller for website and onboarding data described in this policy.

  • Controller: PrivChain
  • Contact email: hello@privchain.io
  • GitHub organization: github.com/PrivChain

Data We Process

  • Waitlist form fields you submit (for example name, email, organization, role, use case details).
  • Operational metadata for abuse prevention and diagnostics (for example timestamp and source page).
  • Optional analytics events only after explicit consent.

Lawful Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent for optional analytics and non-essential cookies.
  • Legitimate interests for service security, fraud prevention, and platform reliability.
  • Pre-contractual steps when handling partner and onboarding requests.

Cookies and Analytics

  • Essential site functionality is active by default.
  • Analytics remains disabled until you click Accept Analytics.
  • You can change your preference at any time using Cookie Settings in the footer.

Retention

  • Waitlist and partner inquiry records are retained only for onboarding and relationship management needs.
  • Data that is no longer needed for these purposes is deleted or anonymized.
  • Security logs are retained for a limited period aligned to abuse-prevention requirements.

Your GDPR Rights

  • Access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, and objection rights where applicable.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior lawful processing).
  • Right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

To exercise rights, email hello@privchain.io with the subject line "Privacy Request".

International Data Transfers

Where personal data is transferred across jurisdictions, PrivChain applies appropriate contractual and organizational safeguards consistent with applicable law.
