Mission and x402 Positioning

PrivChain is a privacy layer for x402-compatible machine payments.

PrivChain does not replace payment transport. PrivChain integrates with x402-compatible HTTP payment flows and provides privacy-preserving verification and settlement capabilities on top.

  • Payment semantics: x402-compatible
  • Privacy layer: PrivChain
  • Primary use case: agent-to-agent and agent-to-service private payments
  • Differentiation: privacy-first SDK and MCP-native tooling
